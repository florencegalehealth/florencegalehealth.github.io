Home Physicals: A healthcare professional sent to your home.

You are busy, and taking time off from work for your annual exam or your child's sport or physical exam is the last thing you need. Florence Gale Health is available 24/7 and will send a healthcare professional to you. No more waiting in the clinic for a 15 minute physical exam.
